Align Services

Our Align services focus on providing solutions for misaligned teeth. Whether it's traditional braces or modern clear aligners, our experienced orthodontists tailor treatment plans to meet individual needs, ensuring a straighter and healthier smile.

Price: Starting from $2,000

Precautions:

  • A consultation with our orthodontist is necessary to determine the most suitable treatment.
  • Patient compliance with wearing aligners or braces is crucial for successful results.
  • Regular follow-up appointments are required for adjustments and progress monitoring.
  • Maintain proper oral hygiene to prevent issues during the alignment process.
