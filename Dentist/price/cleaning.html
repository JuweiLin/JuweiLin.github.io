Regular Cleaning

Regular cleaning, also known as prophylaxis, is essential for maintaining good oral hygiene. This routine procedure is recommended bi-annually to remove plaque buildup, prevent cavities, and keep your gums healthy.

Price: $75

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning, or scaling and root planing, is a more intensive cleaning procedure. It is typically recommended for patients with signs of gum disease and involves removing tartar from above and below the gum line.

Price: $200
